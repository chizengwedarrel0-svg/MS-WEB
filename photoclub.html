Photography workshop

Photography workshops at the MS Therapy Centre are led by service user Nicola Morcombe.

Everyone has fun using their cameras or mobile phone cameras with tips and hints from Nicola.

Sometimes we go to local beauty spots to take outside photos and have some coffee. Accessibility is always considered.
There are spaces for more people to take part in the sessions.

If you are a member of the Centre, volunteer or carer, and would like to take part, please email reception@mscentrebedsandnorthants.com
