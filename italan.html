Italian Spectacular

Saturday 8th August 2026 7pm-10pm

Join us for an evening of Italian music & food in aid of the Bedford MS Therapy Centre

A musical journey through iconic Italian music, reimagined with a sophisticated jazz twist. The quartet brings new life to much-loved Italian songs from iconic Movies such as Cinema Paradiso by Ennio Morricone, The God Father by Nino Rota and many other classic melodies celebrating 'La Dolce Vita'.

Delicious Italian buffet provided by SAPORI – A wide selection of pizzas including vegetarian, antipasti boards with meats, cheeses & olives, served with fresh breads and a selection of mixed salads. Enjoy Italian mignon cakes for dessert
